Principle Concepts Learned

  • 'position: static' is the default position the page tells it to go, seen here
  • 'position: relative' allows to change units of location relative to natural location (aka static position) on the page.
  • 'position: relative' is made up of 4 properties (top, bottom, left, right) that allow you to shift number of units relative to it's ancestor.
