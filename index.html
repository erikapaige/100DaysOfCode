Home (current) Week 1 Bootstrap

Like many people, Covid brought my industry to a halt. The extra time allowed me dig into problems I’ve experienced in freelance styling - like the gap between independent fashion brand’s creative vision and access to tech functionality.

After considering the multiple solutions, I enrolled in the University of Irvine’s 12-week Web Development bootcamp. The program allowed me to acquire skills in full stack web development to communicate a brand’s story. Being out of formal education for several years, committing to coding first meant re-learning classroom style learning. Twelve grueling weeks later, I am a proud coding bootcamp graduate.

In order to maintain and increase my skill level, August 10th, I made the decision to join the #100DaysOfCode challenge. The challenge asks participants to commit to at least 1 hour each day of coding for 100 days. 

I will begin by reviewing what I learned in boot camp to further solidify my foundation. Following that, I will educate myself on platforms relevant to the fashion industry (like Shopify, Amazon, Google Analytics, SEO, and Squarespace, to name a few)

For daily briefings please follow me on twitter (@erikapaigestyle), as the challenge requests that participants tweet their progress everyday. To view my projects and code please visit my GitHub profile: erikapaige. My code will be pushed up daily so that anyone can view my progress.
